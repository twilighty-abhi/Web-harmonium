Learn Harmonium Online (FAQ)

What is Web Harmonium?

Web Harmonium is a free online harmonium you can play in your browser, with lessons and a mentor-style play-along mode.

How many keys does it have?

It features 42 chromatic keys (C3–F6) rendered as realistic ivory and ebony-style keys.

Can I learn harmonium online for free?

Yes. Choose a lesson, follow the phrase-by-phrase mentor flow, and finish with a scored full-run.
